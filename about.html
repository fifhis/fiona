about me

Hi there, I'm a Cognitive Science student at UC San Diego, specializing in Design and Interaction. I'm passionate about creating intuitive user interfaces that make people's lives easier.

During my time in UCSD, I've gained hands-on experience through design courses on campus, where I've honed my skills in various design tools. Moreover, my time interning with previous organizations taught me the importance of putting users first and working collaboratively to solve their problems.

I love diving into the details of user behavior and crafting designs that are not only functional but also a joy to use. I'm always eager to learn and stay updated with the latest design trends. Whether it's mastering new software or refining my existing skills, I’m committed to growing as a designer and pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in user interface design!
