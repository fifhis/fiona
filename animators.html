Animators

We decided to create a prototype for an animation app that integrates tutorials and guides on essential animation principles, the utilization of AI technology, and various features that help beginner animators enjoy their animating journey!

Problem

In current animating apps, users are often left without built-in tutorials within the software. The absence of guidance on animation principles means users must seek information elsewhere, which can be a time-consuming and frustrating process.

Goal

Make it user friendly - Especially for beginners

Our prototype features a straightforward interface for ease of use, particularly for beginners. We integrated tutorials and training on both the animation process and the use of tools and an AI System where Users can utilize AI technology for their animating process.

Wireframes

Prototype

AI Suggeting Frames

To ease the workload for users, our software includes an AI Suggesting Frames feature. This tool generates intermediate frames between two original drawings, significantly reducing the effort required to create numerous animation frames. The animation process is divided into two tasks: "in-betweens" and "key frames."
Users can focus on creating the key frames while the AI handles the in-betweens, ensuring smooth and cohesive animations.

Prototype

Premade Assets and Animation Stylization

Traditional keyframe animation requires meticulous editing of keyframes and in-between frames. Our solution simplifies this by allowing users to use premade objects. Moreover, animation stylizations can be easily applied to the object, bypassing the tedious work and immediately animating the objects.

Prototype

Annotation mode

Annotation mode allows animators to switch between frames and annotate on their animations.

Help Button

The Help Button provides an explanation and a short GIF demonstration of the item user clicks on. There is also a link of the description to guide you to a more detailed explanation.

File Space + Templates

In Templates,users can explore animations created by professionals. They can enter a project, experiment with the work, and gain a deeper understanding of why it works.

Community

Users can also access community videos that demonstrate the animation process and add these videos to their workspace to follow along step-by-step.
