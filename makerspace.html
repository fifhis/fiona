Goal

Faster and Better!

We aim to develop a prototype with features that make the sign-in process more efficient. College students are very busy, and we want to help them skip the long lines and get to their destinations quickly!

Feild Work and Insights

The results of our feild work research showed that 63.9% took over a minute to sign in, 30.6% ignored the kiosk, and 5.8% needed to sign waivers. These findings point to inefficiencies and confusion in the current system, indicating a need for improvements.

Interviews and Insights

We conducted interviews with staff members and students to gain insights into their experiences while navigating the Makerspace. The feedback received from these individuals provided valuable qualitative data about the specific challenges users encounter. This information will guide us in determining the appropriate approaches to address these issues effectively.

This is what we found 👀

1. The check in process is not clear to users.
2. Students cant find their specific destination.
3. Students were unable to sign in without ID.
4. Staff cannot provide help to students efficiently.
5. Students are unable to find staff for help.

Prototype

The redesigned prototype addresses feedback gathered from user interviews by enhancing the sign-in experience. It features a more intuitive interface on the kiosk that guides students through the process: returning users are promptly directed to sign in, while new users are navigated towards waiver signing. Additionally, clear instructions are displayed for both processes, ensuring ease of use. To further assist students, the option to seek help from staff members is prominently incorporated, ensuring a seamless and user-friendly experience..
